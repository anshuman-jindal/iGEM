Welcome to iGEM

The International Genetically Engineered Machine (iGEM) competition is the premiere undergraduate Synthetic Biology competition.

Each year, teams from around the world design and build genetically engineered systems, bringing novel biological functions to life. This year, the competition will be held at Porte de Versailles, Paris from 2-5 November, 2023.
